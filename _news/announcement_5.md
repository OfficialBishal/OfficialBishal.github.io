Submitted **"ARC: Assessment of Interface Residue Conformation using an Ensemble of Graph Neural Networks"** to _PROTEINS: Structure, Function, and Bioinformatics_ (Wiley, Manuscript ID 5704351).

ARC is a first-author project from my participation in **CASP16**, the leading community-wide blind assessment in computational structural biology. The work uses an ensemble of graph neural networks to estimate per-residue local accuracy of predicted protein structures.
