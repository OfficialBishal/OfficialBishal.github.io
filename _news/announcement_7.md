Co-authored paper **"SCW: building the whole-genome 3D structures based on extremely sparse single-cell Hi-C data"** is published in _BMC Bioinformatics_ (Zhu H, Liu T, **Shrestha B**, Wang Z, 2026, 27:90).

SCW reconstructs high-resolution whole-genome 3D structures from extremely sparse single-cell Hi-C data, benchmarked against Nuc_dynamics, Hickit, and Tensor-FLAMINGO across multiple cell types.

Read the paper: [DOI: 10.1186/s12859-026-06421-3](https://doi.org/10.1186/s12859-026-06421-3)
