Co-authored paper **"Inferring the qualities of protein–RNA models with graph transformers"** is published in _Bioinformatics_ (Siciliano AJ, Bao Y, **Shrestha B**, Wang Z, 2026, btag202).

The work extends structure-quality estimation beyond proteins to multi-biomolecule systems by training a graph transformer to assess the accuracy of predicted protein–RNA complex structures.
